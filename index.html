Research

My PhD research covered various topics in Wireless Sensor Networks and Ad Hoc Wireless Networking. In particular, my work focused localized scheduling algorithms for quality of service assurance, energy efficient communication of correlated data, and secure data aggregation in wireless sensor networks. I am currently interested in collaborative e-commerce techniques and distributed e-commerce optimization.
